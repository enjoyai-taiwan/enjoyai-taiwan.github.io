By collecting civilization fragments with robots, young starchasers will trace the footsteps of history, uncovering the creative codes behind these legendary civilizations and discovering how human curiosity shaped the world.

Drone Cup is a dynamic team competition that integrates drone robotics, real-time communication, and intelligent decision-making. Teams must collaboratively pilot drones in a high-speed environment to achieve strategic objectives.

WhalesBot Eagle 125F

建議組別：國小高年級至高中組 (9-18歲)

「星際對決」青少年機器人對戰賽

High-stakes competitive challenge where two teams - Red and Blue - go head-to-head. In the game, team members must communicate clearly, make quick decisions, and adjust their strategy as the situation on the field changes.

WhalesBot AI Module 3s
